about

Sound for products, picture / & interactive systems.

I bring nearly two decades of audio-production experience together with a senior background in UX and product design — so I approach sound as more than decoration: every cue has a state, purpose, hierarchy, and place inside a larger system. I work across interface audio, original score, sound to picture, sonic identity, mixing, restoration, and final delivery — from an early interaction map to implementation-ready assets, making sound feel native to the product, story, and environment around it.
