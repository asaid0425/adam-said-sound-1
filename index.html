01 why sound

sound is part / of the interface.

Most products treat audio as an afterthought. Done well, it does real work — confirming actions, guiding attention, and making an interface feel responsive.

about me

product designer / specialized in sound.

A senior product designer with nearly two decades in audio production. Most sound designers understand audio. Most UX designers understand interaction. I work at the intersection of both.

So I design for feedback, hierarchy, timing, and accessibility — not just how a sound plays in isolation. Every cue has a state, a purpose, and a place in the system, delivered as implementation-ready assets your team can ship.
